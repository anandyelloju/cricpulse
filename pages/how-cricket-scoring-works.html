How cricket scoring works

Cricket scoring follows the match from setup through the final result. A scorer records teams, toss, innings, overs, runs, wickets, extras, and player statistics.

Start with match setup

Choose team names, player count, match overs, toss winner, batting decision, opening batters, and opening bowler.

Score ball by ball

Each delivery updates the team total, batter stats, bowler figures, current over, and innings status. At the end of an over, the scorer selects the next bowler.

Finish with a scorecard

After the match, a scorecard summarizes innings totals, batting highlights, bowling figures, key moments, and final result.
