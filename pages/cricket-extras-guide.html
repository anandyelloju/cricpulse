Cricket extras guide

Extras are runs added to the team total that are not always credited to the batter. CricPulse supports wides, no balls, byes, and leg byes for local cricket scoring.

Wides and no balls

A wide or no ball normally adds one extra run and does not count as a legal delivery. Additional runs can be recorded when batters run or the ball reaches the boundary.

Byes and leg byes

Byes and leg byes are added to the team score but not the batter score. They usually count as legal deliveries unless combined with another illegal delivery call.

Why extras matter

Accurate extras keep totals, bowler figures, over history, and match summaries trustworthy.
